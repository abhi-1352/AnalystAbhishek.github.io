01 : About me

My Story

Hello! I'm Abhishek Kumar Singh, a Data Analyst with a strong foundation in computer science and a deep passion for data analytics. I completed my Bachelor's in Computer Science from Gujarat Technological University, where I honed my skills in programming and problem-solving.

After graduation, I initially pursued Android development. I developed and published my own app on the Google Play Store, gaining valuable hands-on experience in building, deploying, and maintaining software. However, after a couple of years, I realized I wanted to take a step back and consider the direction I truly wanted to pursue in my career. During this two-year period, I explored different fields and reflected on my interests.

It was during this time that I discovered Data Analytics, a field that instantly intrigued me. I was captivated by the power of turning raw data into actionable insights. To back this newfound interest, I completed a 6-month certification in Data Analytics, where I learned essential skills in Excel, Python, SQL, Power BI, and data cleaning and manipulation. These tools have equipped me with the knowledge to analyze data efficiently and effectively, enabling me to solve complex problems through data-driven insights.

I am now actively seeking opportunities to apply my skills and continue growing as a Data Analyst. If you’re looking for someone who’s passionate about analytics, eager to learn, and dedicated to delivering results, I’d love to connect.

Feel free to reach out—I'm ready to take on new challenges and contribute to meaningful projects!

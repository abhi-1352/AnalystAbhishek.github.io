001 : AdventureWorks Sales Project

The AdventureWorks Sales Power BI report analyzes sales data over the past two years across various product categories. It provides insights into sales trends, top-performing products, and city-level performance, helping identify growth opportunities. The report uses interactive visualizations, filters, and KPIs to deliver actionable insights. Data cleaning and transformation were applied to ensure accuracy and consistency for business decision-making.

Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze sales data to gain insights from two perspectives: customer demographics and product categories. It aims to identify trends in customer purchasing behavior and segment customers based on key attributes. Additionally, the project will evaluate product performance, highlighting top-selling items and seasonal patterns. The goal is to provide actionable insights to optimize marketing strategies and inventory management. Ultimately, the project will support data-driven decision-making for enhanced sales performance.

Methodology


Data Transformation:

The data transformation process involved the following steps:

  • Data Import: The raw data was imported into SQL Server Management Studio (SSMS) for analysis.
  • Column Removal: Unnecessary columns that did not contribute to the analysis were removed to streamline the dataset.
  • Handling Null Values: Missing values were addressed by filling NULL entries with appropriate defaults or calculated values, ensuring completeness and accuracy.
  • Data Filtering: Filters were applied to extract only the sales data from the last two years, relative to the current date, to focus on recent performance.
  • Data Preparation: These steps ensured the dataset was clean, relevant, and ready for further analysis.

Data Modeling and Measures:

  • Measures Creation using DAX: Created DAX measures to calculate Total Sales, Total Budget, and Profit/Loss.
  • KPI Creation: Designed Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) such as Total Sales to track overall sales performance.
  • Dynamic Filtering: KPIs were made filterable by various dimensions, including Year, Month, Product, Category, and Sub-category to allow detailed, segmented analysis.
  • Product Category Analysis: Developed KPIs to analyze sales by Product Category and Sub-category, helping to identify performance trends at different levels of the product hierarchy.
  • Top Selling Products: Created KPIs to display the Top 10 Selling Products, offering insights into the highest-performing products.

1. Visualization Creation:

  • Tool Used: Visualizations were created using Power BI to build interactive dashboards.
  • Visualizations:
    • Line Charts were used to display sales trends over time.
    • Bar Graphs were used to compare sales performance across products, categories, and other dimensions.
    • Donut Charts were used to show the proportion of sales across different segments.
    • Pie Charts were employed to visualize the distribution of sales by product category.
    • A Matrix Table was included to provide a detailed view of sales data across multiple dimensions (e.g., year, month, product).
  • Interactive Filters: Filters were added to allow users to dynamically drill down and analyze data by year, month, product, and category, enabling flexible data exploration.
  • Goal: The objective was to present the data in an easily interpretable format, supporting quick decision-making and offering actionable insights.

2. Dashboard Development:

  • Dashboards:
    • Sales Overview Dashboard: Provided a high-level view of total sales, profit/loss, and key performance metrics.
    • Customer Details Dashboard: Focused on customer segments, demographics, and purchasing behaviors, offering insights into customer-driven sales patterns.
    • Product Details Dashboard: Showed product-level performance, highlighting top-selling products, product categories, and sales trends.
  • Interactive Features: Dashboards were designed to be filterable, allowing users to drill down into specific details by year, month, category, product, and customer segment.
  • Goal: The goal was to create comprehensive, user-friendly dashboards that provided quick insights into different aspects of the sales process, enabling data-driven decision-making.
